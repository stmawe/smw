Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to use?

Yes! Browsing and creating an account is completely free. We only take a small commission on successful sales.

How do I know it's safe?

All users are verified through their university email. Transactions are protected and we recommend meeting in public places.

Can I sell anything?

You can sell most items, but we prohibit illegal goods and items that violate university policies.

What if I have a problem?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help resolve disputes and answer any questions.
